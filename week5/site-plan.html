About Our Organization

Description

The name represents a non-profit organization dedicated to assisting vulnerable communities, social development, and cultural promotion. Its name reflects its commitment to art as a tool for social transformation and its focus on building a more just society.

Domain availability: arteyconstruccionsocial.org (pending verification)

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an information and engagement platform to:

  • Educate visitors about the organization's mission and programs
  • Provide legal, educational, and social service resources
  • Promote cultural and artistic events
  • Encourage volunteering and community involvement
  • Offer secure options for donations and project funding
  • Share beneficiary stories to generate connection and inspiration

User Scenarios

Key questions site visitors might have:

  • How can I become a volunteer to help with community projects?
  • What are the upcoming cultural events and how can I attend?
  • How can I make a donation and how will the money raised be used?
  • Where can I find legal and educational resources for communities in vulnerable situations?

Color Scheme

Colors used:

  • Dark Blue (#002a5c) - Used in headers and footers to give a professional appearance
  • Gold (#ffcc00) - Used in action buttons such as "Donate" or "Join"
  • White (#ffffff) - Used in backgrounds to improve readability
  • Light Gray (#f4f4f4) - For informational sections

Typography

Poppins: For the overall content of the site, providing a modern and legible appearance.

Roboto (bold): Used in titles and headings for greater visual impact.

Planned Features

  • Homepage: General information about the organization, its mission, and recent updates
  • About Us: History, team, and organizational structure
  • Programs and Services: Detailed description of social assistance projects
  • Events and News: Calendar of activities and featured publications
  • Get Involved: Ways to participate, volunteer, and collaborate
  • Donate: Secure donation options and transparency reports
  • Testimonials: Stories of impact shared by beneficiaries
  • Contact: Contact form and social media links
